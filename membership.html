Becoming a member

PADCDI is Philippines' industry network for businesses implementing or evaluating blockchain or distributed ledger technology.

As part of the PADCDI network, individual businesses may:

  • engage with and learn from other businesses implementing blockchain solutions;
  • share best-practice and collaborate as appropriate;
  • access national and global thought-leaders;
  • contribute to PADCDIâ€™s advocacy agenda with key government and regulatory stakeholders; and
  • participate in PADCDI conferences, workshops and study tours.

Collectively, PADCDI members help advance the adoption of blockchain technology in the Philippines and work together to advocate for appropriate regulatory and policy settings.

Industry members

Industry members are businesses that are directly implementing blockchain or distributed ledger technology within their business or planning to do so.

Partner members

Partner members are businesses that provide advice including technical, legal or commercial consulting services to other businesses.
